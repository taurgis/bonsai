## Summary

Troubleshoot memory issues > Instructions seem lost after `/compact`

## Compressed

### Instructions seem lost after `/compact`

Project-root CLAUDE.md survives compaction: after `/compact`, Claude re-reads it from disk and re-injects it into the session. Nested CLAUDE.md files in subdirectories are not re-injected automatically; they reload the next time Claude reads a file in that subdirectory. If an instruction disappeared after compaction, it was either given only in conversation or lives in a nested CLAUDE.md that hasn’t reloaded yet. Add conversation-only instructions to CLAUDE.md to make them persist. See [What survives compaction] for the full breakdown. See [Write effective instructions] for guidance on size, structure, and specificity.
